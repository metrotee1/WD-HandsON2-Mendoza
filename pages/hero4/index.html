Ninja ZX6RR


The whole new Ninja ZX-6R was designed to embody superior performance on track, and on the street. The 636 engine – unique to this motorcycle, was engineered to deliver strong and balanced power at all RPM ranges. Electronics that were only previously found on the Ninja ZX-10R and Ninja ZX-10RR such as Power Mode Selector, KTRC, KQS and KIBS, balance out this bike’s race-track worthy power, with street-ready safety features.
